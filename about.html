about us

The UESTC Guitar Association is a community of interests organized by the students of software & information. The third presidents are ZhanXu and Zhang Jianhua. During their tenure, UESTC Guitar Association reached 200 and held many major events and honors.
The main activity of the association is communicate of guitar tracks and band play.
